MARTIN QUAD

MARTIN QUAD's DEBUT AT CPHFW EMANATES FROM HIS OWN OBSESSION WITH THE THEMES DESTRUCTION AND TRADITION. THIS DEBUT COLLECTION & PRESTENTATIONS PLAYS WITH THE WAY THAT A CATWALK IS TRADITIONALLY ORCHESTRATED, BY CONSOLIDATION COMPONENTS FROM THE OPERA AND EXPERIMENTING WITH PUSHING THE BOUNDARIES FOR CREATIVE EXPRESSION, IN THE FASHION INDUSTRY.

THE INTERPLAY BETWEEN MULTIPLE INDUSTRIES OPENS NEW WAYS OF DISPLAYING AND UNDERSTANDING FASHION. IT IS MARTIN QUAD's MISSION TO OPEN THE DOOR TO A GREATER CULUTRAL COGERENCE IN FASHION.

MARTIN QUAD WORKS BY YEARLY THEMES AND FOR MARTIN QUAD's DEBUT YEAR, THE THEME IS "OPERA".
